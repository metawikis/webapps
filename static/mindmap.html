Mind Map of the Relationship Between Students and Teachers

Students and Teachers

This is a mind map of the relationship between students and teachers.

Students

  • Learn
    The primary goal of students is to learn.

    Students learn by listening to lectures, reading textbooks, doing homework, and participating in class discussions.

  • Grow
    Students also grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

    Students grow intellectually by learning new concepts and ideas. They grow emotionally by learning to manage their emotions and by developing relationships with their peers and teachers. They grow socially by learning to interact with others and by developing their own identity.

  • Become
    Ultimately, the goal of students is to become well-rounded individuals who are prepared for college, career, and life.

    Students become well-rounded individuals by developing their academic, intellectual, emotional, and social skills.

Teachers

  • Teach
    The primary goal of teachers is to teach.

    Teachers teach by delivering lectures, assigning homework, and leading class discussions.

  • Support
